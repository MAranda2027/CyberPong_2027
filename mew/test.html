Mechanics

General Description of Game:

-CyberPong 2027 immerses players in a neon-drenched, cyberpunk world where paddles clash and pixels flash.

-Developed by Rebooted Realms Interactive, the game offers a high-octane arcade experience with three adrenaline-pumping levels.

-Each level features distinct challenges and aesthetics inspired by the iconic CyberPunk 2077 universe.

-It is an elevated and reimagined experience of the arcade classic Pong.

-The rules are simple, just use the controls down below to knock the Pong into the opponent's wall to score a point. First to 10 wins.-

-There are also debuffs inspired by the famous CyberPunk 2077-

Controls:

- Players navigate the game using intuitive controls, including W, S, arrow up, and arrow down keys.

- These controls ensure seamless gameplay, allowing players to focus on the action-packed battles without interruption.

Debuffs:

EMP-BLAST: Releases an electromagnetic pulse that temporarily scrambles your opponent's controls, making their paddle move erratically.

Reprogram: This powerup allows you to reverse the controls of your opponent's paddle for a short period, causing confusion.

Data Distortion: This power-up causes the pixels on your opponent's side of the screen to shift and warp, creating visual chaos and potentially causing them to miss the ball.
